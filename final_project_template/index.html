Welcome to San Juan Island Hostel

Have you been looking for a place to stay in Friday Harbor?

San Juan Island Hostel is just minutes away from the ferry terminal in Friday Harbor and allows you to stay for a short time while visiting the island, or for a longer time while working here. If you are on a budget and looking for a place to lay your head or just enjoy meeting new people, this is the perfect place for you. We offer private dorms, shared dorms, and a group dorm if you are coming with your friends. You will also have access to our brand new amenities, including a full kitchen, to make your stay as convenient as possible.

Our hostel is in walking distance of all the restaurants, bars, shops, and businesses in Friday Harbor. It is also easy to get to anywhere on the rest of the island from our location. Stay with us and explore what beautiful San Juan Island has to offer!
